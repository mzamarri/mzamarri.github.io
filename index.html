My Journey

Still Learning. Still Building.

Computers have always peaked my interest, so after being laid off back at the very end of 2022 from Qualcomm, I found myself at a crossroads in my career. While I enjoyed learning about the low-level details of a computer system, over time I found myself increasingly drawn to software for its ability to transform ideas into products that solve real problems. As my interest in entrepreneurship grew, choosing software development felt like the natural path. Since then, I’ve spent countless hours teaching myself through online courses, documentation, and hands-on projects. Every project has been another opportunity to learn, improve, and become a more thoughtful developer.

Outside of programming, I’m someone who enjoys challenges that require patience, strategy, and continuous growth. Whether I’m practicing Muay Thai, playing chess, or tackling a difficult technical problem, I enjoy the process of improving a little every day. Today, I'm excited to bring that same curiosity, persistence, and drive to a collaborative engineering team where I can continue growing while making meaningful contributions from day one.
